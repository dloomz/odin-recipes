Pumpkin Pie

Golden pie on a plate.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1 1/4 cup of plain flour
  • 2 tsp caster sugar
  • 1/2 tsp table salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 125g unsalted butter (fridge temp) (or vegetable shortening)
  • 1/4 cup cold water

Filling:

  • 1 425g can of pumpkin puree (or 425g pumpkin flesh!)
  • 200g maple syrup
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp grated nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla essence

Steps

  1. Mix together all dry ingredients and work in the butter
  2. Add cold water and continue to work until smooth
  3. Press dough consistently to cover pie dish
  4. Bake crust for 15 minutes
  5. Pour pumpkin mix into a pan and add syrup to sweeten
  6. Add all spices and vanilla essence
  7. Whisk in evaporated milk and eggs
  8. Pour mix into crust and bake for 15 minutes
