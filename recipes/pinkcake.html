Pink Cake

Cake covered in pink buttercream, topped with strawberries.

In Stardew Valley, the pink cake is a delectable treat that's as visually pleasing as it is delicious. Crafted with care and precision, this confectionary delight boasts a moist and tender crumb, infused with a subtle hint of vanilla essence. What truly sets the pink cake apart, however, is its enchanting hue; a soft, pastel pink that's as charming as a spring blossom. With each slice, players are treated to a burst of sweetness that lingers on the palate, leaving them craving just one more bite.

Whether enjoyed as a decadent dessert after a long day of farming or shared with friends during a festive celebration, the Stardew Valley pink cake is a symbol of comfort and joy in the bustling world of Pelican Town. Its delicate flavor and enchanting appearance make it a favorite among residents and players alike, evoking a sense of warmth and happiness with every mouthful.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Whip the eggs and sugar
  2. Gradually add flour
  3. Add hot melted butter to milk
  4. Mix some batter into hot milk
  5. Slowly pour milk mixture back into whipped eggs
  6. Pour into 2 cake pans
  7. Knock out big air bubbles
  8. Bake 30 minutes
  9. Cool upside down
  10. Decorate
