Parsnip Soup

Ingredients

  • 3 parsnips
  • 3-4 garlic cloves
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 stick celery
  • 2tbsp cider vinegar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • Olive oil for frying
  • 1 vegetable stock cube
  • 2 tsp ground tumeric
  • 4-5 sprigs thyme
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp ground coriander
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Prep vegetables
  2. Set oil on a low heat and add the spices
  3. Add in the onions,garlic and celery
  4. Chop parsnips into discs
  5. Add parsnips, thyme leaves and plenty of salt to pan
  6. Add 1 tbsp of vinegar and stir everything
  7. Add the stock cube, 1 1/2 cups of hot water and 1 cup of milk
  8. Allow to simmer for 30 minutes
  9. Blend mixture
