Parsnip Soup

Bowl filled with yellow-coloured soup.

In the quaint village of Pelican Town, the parsnip soup stands as a humble yet heartwarming culinary creation. Crafted from the freshest ingredients harvested straight from the fertile fields, this soup embodies the essence of rustic charm. With its simple yet robust flavor profile, the parsnip soup exudes a comforting warmth that soothes the soul on even the chilliest of days.

Each spoonful of this savory concoction is a journey through layers of flavor, from the earthy sweetness of the parsnips to the subtle hints of aromatic herbs and spices. Whether enjoyed as a nourishing meal on a crisp autumn afternoon or served as a starter to a hearty feast, the parsnip soup is a beloved staple in the kitchens of Pelican Town. Its unassuming nature belies its ability to evoke a sense of home and belonging, making it a cherished favorite among villagers and players alike.

Ingredients

  • 3 parsnips
  • 3-4 garlic cloves
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 stick celery
  • 2tbsp cider vinegar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • Olive oil for frying
  • 1 vegetable stock cube
  • 2 tsp ground tumeric
  • 4-5 sprigs thyme
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp ground coriander
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Prep vegetables
  2. Set oil on a low heat and add the spices
  3. Add in the onions,garlic and celery
  4. Chop parsnips into discs
  5. Add parsnips, thyme leaves and plenty of salt to pan
  6. Add 1 tbsp of vinegar and stir everything
  7. Add the stock cube, 1 1/2 cups of hot water and 1 cup of milk
  8. Allow to simmer for 30 minutes
  9. Blend mixture
